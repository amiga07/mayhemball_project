Of Velvet And Vice

The track was written and produced by Gaga with Andrew Watt and Cirkut. A part of the song uses the melody of "Spellbound" by Siouxsie and the Banshees and written by Siouxsie Sioux (Susan Ballion), Budgie (Peter Edward Clarke), John McGeoch and Steven Severin (Steven John Bailey).
