"Die With a Smile" is a song recorded by Lady Gaga and Bruno Mars. It was released on August 16th, 2024 through Streamline and Interscope Records. The song was written by Bruno, Gaga, Dernst “D'Mile” Emile II, Andrew Watt and James Fauntleroy while production was handled by the former four. It appears on Gaga's seventh studio album, Mayhem, as the fourteenth track.
